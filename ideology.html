Abhinandanism

Abhinandanism or Dominionism is a Third Positionist political ideology that synthesizes elements of patriotic nationalism, militarism, and civilizational revivalism rooted in Sanatan Dharma. It advocates for a centralized, authoritarianform of governance led by a morally upright and strong military leader, with the core aim of restoring India’s historical and spiritual greatness. The ideology positions itself as a post-democratic, post-colonial response to perceived internal divisions and external threats to national sovereignty and cultural integrity. It combines cultural traditionalism with statist authoritarianism, rejecting both liberal democratic pluralism and Marxist collectivism.

The ideology promotes the awakening of civilizational consciousness, encouraging individuals to align personal growth with national and cultural resurgence.

  • Self-discipline and inner strength
  • Cultural pride and preservation
  • Collective unity over division
  • Strategic and long-term thinking
