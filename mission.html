Rising With Sanatan Organisation's Mission

We Hindus are the original inhabitants of Bharat, and we have been subjugated for centuries. Our mission is to reclaim our rightful place as the rulers of our land, to restore our cultural heritage, and to build a future where our children can thrive in a society that honors our traditions and values. We seek to unite all Hindus under a common banner, to resist foreign influences, and to create a strong, prosperous, and self-reliant nation that reflects the glory of our ancient civilization.
